FAQ

1. Is it possible to place a single order for multiple children?

Yes! Click on "Did you get another access code?" under the main menu login screen. You can enter up to five access codes at the same time. This will give you access to all available photos and you can save on shipping costs!

2. Where can I see the status of my order?

The easiest way is to click the link within your order confirmation email. Another option is you can log in with the password and email address you received via email, then your order details can be found under "My Account".

3. Can I crop the images?

Yes! You will find this option within the shopping cart when you are checking out. Look for the stars which indicate whether the photo's resolution is sufficient for the enlargement.

4. How long will it take to get my order?

Most orders will take 1-2 weeks to be shipped. Digital images will be sent immediately after payment has been processed, unless specified otherwise.

5. It says my digital image was sent, but it's not in my inbox.

It's always a safe bet to check your spam folder! If it's not there, and you have verified you entered the correct email address, please send an email to customerservice@pmiphoto.com.
